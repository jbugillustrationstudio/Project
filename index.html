Did you know?

Familial hypercholesterolemia (FH) is a genetic condition affecting 1 in 250 people worldwide, causing dangerously high cholesterol from birth. Most remain undiagnosed until adulthood, yet the process starts at birth—putting you at risk of heart attack in men under 55 and women under 60.

 

By integrating research directly into patient care, we offer eligible patients the opportunity to participate in cutting-edge studies. If you meet the criteria, you can take part in clinical trials and research initiatives that help shape the future of cardiovascular medicine—gaining access to advanced therapies, innovative diagnostics, and personalised treatment strategies.
