Referral Information for Medical Practitioners:

We welcome referrals and clinical correspondence from medical practitioners. Our preferred method of communication is Medical Objects, which allows for secure and efficient transfer of patient information. Alternatively, referrals and correspondence can be sent via email, which is our preferred option over fax for faster and more reliable processing:

Email: info@cholesterolcare.com.au

Fax: (07) 3740 4777

Please include all relevant clinical information, investigation results, and patient contact details to assist with timely assessment and management. If you have any questions regarding the referral process, feel free to contact our team.
