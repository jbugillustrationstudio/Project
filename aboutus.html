About Us

At Cholesterol Care Australia, we deliver specialist cardiovascular care with a refined focus on prevention, precision, and long-term health outcomes. Our clinic is built on the belief that exceptional care combines clinical expertise with time, attention, and a genuinely personalised approach. We are committed to creating a calm, professional environment where patients feel informed, supported, and confident in every stage of their care.

A/Prof Karam Kostner

is an Associate Professor of Medicine at the University of Queensland and Director of Cardiology at Mater Hospitals in Brisbane, specialising in preventive cardiology and lipid disorders. He is a highly experienced lipidologist and cardiovascular researcher, having led major clinical trials, published extensively, advised government bodies and held prominent editorial and leadership roles in national and international cardiology organisations.

We are dedicated in delivering a standard of care defined by excellence, discretion, and attention to detail. Our commitment is reflected in:

  • Personalised, precision-led care tailored to each patient
  • Clinical excellence grounded in the latest research and best practice
  • A refined patient experience, where your time and comfort are respected
  • Clarity and transparency, ensuring you feel informed and confident in your care
  • Continuity and trust, building long-term relationships focused on your health outcomes
