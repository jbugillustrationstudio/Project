Arrival

It is recommended to arrive at least 15 minutes before your scheduled appointment to allow for parking and check-in procedures.

Parking

The Taylor Medical Center has its own parking lot, which is accessible at 7 Catherine Street. Additionally, there are public parking spaces available on the surrounding streets.

Public Transport

The Taylor Medical Center is 7 minutes walk (450m) from the Mater Hill bus station. Bus routes 197 and 116 stop in front of the medical center.

Accessibility Information

The clinic is accessible via lift and has wheelchair access.
